### What's new
Wow! It's been **2 incredible years** since Ankimon started, and what better way to celebrate than with the massive **v2 release**! 🎉 This version brings a game-changing **SQLite storage migration**, the highly requested **Generation 9 Pokémon**, and a handy **in-app updater**. Plus, you can now boost your progress with the brand new **Lucky Egg** feature alongside a ton of UI fixes and balance improvements. Get ready to catch 'em all like never before! 🚀✨
